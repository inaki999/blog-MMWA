Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how mmwa.es uses them, and your choices regarding their use. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described here.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They help websites remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and analyze traffic to improve your experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Necessary Essential for basic site navigation and security.
  • Analytics To understand how you use mmwa.es and improve our content.
  • Advertising To show relevant ads through Google AdSense based on your interests.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that also set cookies on your device:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us measure how users interact with our site content.
  • Google AdSense: Uses cookies to serve personalized ads based on your visits to mmwa.es and other sites.

4. Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can delete existing cookies and block new ones at any time. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of mmwa.es.

For more information on managing cookies, visit aboutcookies.org.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at support.digitalweb@gmail.com.
