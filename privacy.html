Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At mmwa.es, accessible from https://mmwa.es, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by mmwa.es and how we use it.

1. Log Files

We follow a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected includes IP addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, and referring pages. This information is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

2. Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of the third-party vendors on our site. It uses DART cookies to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to mmwa.es and other sites on the internet.

Visitors may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at: Google Ads Policy

3. Advertising Partners

Third-party ad servers use technologies like cookies or Web Beacons that are used in their respective advertisements appearing on mmwa.es. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. These are used to measure effectiveness and personalize content.

4. GDPR Data Protection Rights

We want to ensure you are fully aware of your rights. Every user is entitled to:

  • Access: Request copies of your data.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data under certain conditions.

5. Contact Information

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support.digitalweb@gmail.com.
