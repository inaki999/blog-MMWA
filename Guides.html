Phase 01: Setup

1. Technical Foundations & Security

Most beginners lose their first $50 due to "High-Risk" flags in payment gateways. You must establish trust with servers from day one.

  • Verified PayPal Tier 2: Link a real bank account and complete the photo ID check.
  • Dedicated IP Integrity: Never use a free VPN. Sites track IP reputation.
  • Anti-Phishing Layer: Use a dedicated physical security key or Google Authenticator.

Apps don't send tasks randomly; they send them to "High-Quality Respondents."
